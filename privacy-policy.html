Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

At REMIA AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when using our API for compliance with Industry Awards, wage calculations, and other HR-related services. We are compliant with Australia's Privacy Act 1988, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable, and other relevant regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary for providing our services, including:

  • Employee data (e.g., name, job role, wage rates, work hours, entitlements).
  • Business details (e.g., company name, industry, location).
  • Compliance-related data (Industry Award information, leave entitlements, penalties).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate wage calculations, overtime, leave entitlements, and compliance with Industry Awards.
  • Improve our API’s performance and accuracy over time through aggregate insights.

3. Legal Basis for Data Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: Explicit consent from businesses or employees for processing personal data.
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing necessary to fulfill contractual obligations (e.g., wage calculations for compliance).

4. Data Security

We prioritize data security by implementing the following measures:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using SSL/TLS and AES-256 encryption standards.
  • Access Control: We implement strict access control, ensuring only authorized personnel can access sensitive information.
  • Data Anonymization: Where feasible, personal data is anonymized to minimize privacy risks.

5. Data Retention Policy

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements. You can request data deletion at any time:

  • Employee and business data is retained as long as required for service delivery and compliance.
  • Data is deleted upon request or when the business terminates its use of our services.

6. Sharing of Data

We may share data with trusted third-party providers when necessary for service delivery:

  • We do not sell or share personal information for marketing purposes.
  • We may share data with payroll systems, cloud service providers, and legal advisors for legitimate purposes.

7. User Rights

You have the following rights concerning your data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correct: You can request corrections if any of the data is inaccurate.
  • Right to Delete: You can request the deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitations on how your data is used.

8. Compliance with Laws

We comply with relevant privacy laws, including:

  • Australia’s Privacy Act 1988: Governs the collection, use, and management of personal information in Australia.
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): Applies if you are operating in or handling data from the European Union.
  • Fair Work Act: Ensures compliance with Australian Industry Awards regulations.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected businesses and employees within 72 hours of discovery.
  • Provide details on the nature of the breach and steps taken to mitigate its impact.

10. Consent Mechanism

We obtain consent for data collection and processing through:

  • Opt-in agreements during account creation and service use.
  • Consent requests when sensitive information is uploaded into the API.
